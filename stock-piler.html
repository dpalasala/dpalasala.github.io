Robinhood Integration

Stock Piler does not place Robinhood trades. It prepares your research and opens the symbol page so you can review and place orders manually inside Robinhood.

Supported in Stock Piler U.S. stocks, ETFs, ADRs

These match the main investment types Robinhood supports for a U.S. investing account.

Not automated Buy and sell orders

Keep real-money order entry in Robinhood until there is a supported official API path.

Safe Robinhood Workflow

  1. Use Stock Piler to choose a candidate and monthly amount.
  2. Open the symbol in Robinhood from the watchlist.
  3. Confirm the symbol, current price, and fractional-share availability.
  4. Place the buy manually.
  5. Add stop-loss or stop-limit protection manually when appropriate.

Monthly Robinhood Checklist

  1. Open Robinhood and confirm the symbol is available.
  2. Place the monthly buy manually using your planned amount.
  3. After the order fills, record your average price.
  4. Create a stop-loss or stop-limit rule if the position needs downside protection.
  5. Review allocation once a month, not every minute.
